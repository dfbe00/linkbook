LinkBook


The social media for links!
Linkbook is a social link manager which lets you manage your links as well as lets you share them with your followers.

Other cool features of LinkBook:
Whom to follow?
You can always see recommendor page to get a list of users whom you can follow.
What's trending?
You can get an animated cloud of most popular tags on LinkBook at our trending page.
Where are the link I upvoted?
You can always find the links you upvoted here for future reference.
How to manage my links?
You can manage all your links here. You can import or remove any link to/from any book.
